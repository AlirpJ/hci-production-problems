Typefaces: Dongle and Nunito.

These are both sans-serif fonts. Characterized by their bold roundness, they are playful and rhythmic!

Font: Dongle.
Dongle is a sans serif font known for its playful and rhythmic display. Created by Yanghee Ryu, it is named after a Korean onomatopoeia meaning "rounded or curved"!
Font: Nunito.
Nunito is a well-balanced, rounded sans serif font. Nunito is best suited for display typography and created by artist Vernon Adams. It was picked with Dongle because they are both round sans serif fonts that are distinct yet balanced.
